Assessing the Role of Personality in Drug Usage

Is there an association between the usage of legal and illegal drugs and quantitatively measured personality traits? We combine a large survey dataset with clinical personality scores to explore how traits like openness, impulsivity, and sensation seeking relate to drug use.
